Our Core Services

Robotic Process Automation

Robotic process automation (RPA) uses software robots to automate repetitive, rule-based tasks like data entry and system integration. It mimics human actions in digital systems to work quickly and accurately. Today, millions of robots are at work around the world, touching virtually every industry and business process.

Custom Software Development

Custom software development creates tailored applications designed specifically for your business needs. Unlike off-the-shelf solutions, it delivers exactly the functionality, scalability, and integration you require. From streamlining internal operations to building customer-facing platforms, custom software drives efficiency and competitive advantage.

Enterprise Integration

Enterprise integration connects disparate systems, applications, and data sources into a unified ecosystem. It eliminates silos by enabling seamless data flow across cloud platforms, legacy systems, and third-party services. This unified approach optimizes operations, reduces errors, and provides real-time insights across your entire organization.

Quantum Pulse

QuantumPulse AB is a Swedish automation consultancy focused on turning manual, time-consuming processes into reliable, scalable digital workflows. We combine deep experience in RPA, software development, and AI to help businesses work faster, smarter, and with fewer errors.

With over 15 years in software engineering and enterprise automation, we design and build end-to-end solutions using platforms like UiPath, Azure, and modern web technologies. Our work spans finance, HR, and operations, where we streamline invoice handling, data processing, and system integrations.

We work closely with each client to understand their processes, then deliver automations that are secure, maintainable, and ready to grow with the business. Whether you need a proof-of-concept, a production-ready robot, or a full automation strategy, QuantumPulse AB is your partner from idea to implementation.

QuantumPulse AB is based in Täby, Stockholm, and serves clients across Sweden and internationally. If you are exploring how automation and AI agents can transform your organization, we would be happy to discuss your needs and share concrete examples tailored to your situation.
